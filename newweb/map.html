Map and Directions

Directions to Our Office

By Car: Drive north from Kigali City Center via KN 4 and KG 7 Avenues toward Kacyiru, then turn left onto KG 9 Avenue to reach the Go Trip Headquarters.

By Public Transport: Take a bus from Kigali City Center to Kacyiru, get off near the Ministry of Health, and walk along KG 9 Avenue to reach the Go Trip Headquarters.

By Walking: Walk north from Kigali City Center along KN 4 Avenue, continue on KG 7 Avenue, then turn left onto KG 9 Avenue to reach the Go Trip Headquarters in Kacyiru.

Nearby Attractions and Amenities

Nearby Attractions

  • Central Park:A large public park offering walking trails, picnic areas, and beautiful scenery.
  • City Museum: A museum showcasing local history and culture with various exhibits and events.
  • Landmark Tower: A popular landmark offering panoramic views of the city from its observation deck.

Amenities

  • Starbucks: A nearby cafe offering coffee, snacks, and free Wi-Fi.
  • Hotel Plaza: A comfortable hotel with modern amenities and convenient access to our office.
  • Parking Garage: A secure parking facility located just a block away from our office.
